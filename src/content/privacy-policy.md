# Privacy Policy

**HueType** · huetype.dev
Last updated: July 1, 2026

---

## The short version

HueType does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. There are no accounts, no sign-ups, and no servers receiving your data. Everything you do in HueType — saving presets, customizing content, favoriting combos — stays entirely on your own device. We have no way to see it, and we never will.

If you want the full legal picture, read on.

---

## 1. Who we are

HueType is a design tool for evaluating color palette and font pairings. It is operated as an independent side project. You can reach us at hello@huetype.dev.

---

## 2. What data we collect

**We collect no personal data.**

Specifically, HueType does not collect:

- Your name, email address, or any contact information
- Account credentials (there are no accounts)
- IP addresses or device identifiers
- Location data
- Usage analytics or behavioral tracking
- Payment or billing information (HueType is free to use)
- Any content you type into the app (company names, headlines, product descriptions, etc.)

---

## 3. How your preferences are stored

When you save a preset, favorite a combo, or customize content inside HueType, that data is stored in your browser's **localStorage** — a built-in feature of your web browser that keeps data locally on your device.

What this means in practice:

- Your saved data lives on your device, in your browser, and nowhere else.
- We cannot access it. No one can access it except you, through your browser.
- If you clear your browser's localStorage or site data, your saved preferences will be deleted. We cannot recover them.
- If you use HueType in a different browser or on a different device, your saved preferences will not carry over, because they are stored locally on the original device only.
- localStorage is not a cookie and does not transmit data to any server.

---

## 4. Cookies

HueType does not use cookies for tracking, advertising, or analytics purposes.

Your browser may store a small number of technical entries (such as session state) as part of normal web application operation. These are functional only, contain no personal information, and are not accessible to us.

---

## 5. Third-party services

HueType loads fonts from **Google Fonts** (fonts.googleapis.com and fonts.gstatic.com) to render font pairings in the live preview. When your browser requests a font file from Google Fonts, Google may log that request as part of their standard infrastructure operations. This is governed by [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy), not ours.

We do not use:

- Advertising networks
- Analytics platforms (Google Analytics, Mixpanel, Hotjar, etc.)
- Social media tracking pixels
- Third-party authentication services
- Payment processors

If we add any third-party services in the future, this policy will be updated to reflect them before they are introduced.

---

## 6. Children's privacy

HueType is a general-purpose design tool with no age restrictions and no features directed at children. Because we collect no personal data from any user, there is no special handling required for users under 13 (COPPA) or under 16 (GDPR Article 8).

---

## 7. Your rights

Because we hold no personal data about you, most standard data rights (access, rectification, erasure, portability) are not applicable — there is nothing for us to retrieve, correct, delete, or transfer.

If you have used the contact form or emailed hello@huetype.dev and would like us to delete any correspondence we may have received, contact us at hello@huetype.dev and we will do so promptly.

**GDPR (EU/EEA users):** HueType does not process personal data as defined under the General Data Protection Regulation. No legal basis for processing is required because no processing occurs.

**CCPA (California users):** HueType does not sell personal information. We do not collect personal information as defined under the California Consumer Privacy Act.

**PIPEDA (Canadian users):** No personal information is collected, used, or disclosed.

---

## 8. Data security

Because no personal data is transmitted to or stored on our servers, there is no server-side data at risk of breach. Your locally stored preferences are protected by your browser's built-in security model, which prevents other websites from accessing HueType's localStorage data.

---

## 9. External links

HueType may contain links to external resources such as Google Fonts documentation or design references. These sites have their own privacy policies, and we are not responsible for their data practices. We recommend reviewing the privacy policy of any external site you visit.

If we make material changes to this policy — for example, if we add analytics, introduce accounts, or begin using new third-party services — we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where feasible, post a notice within the app.

We will never introduce data collection practices that contradict the core principle of this policy (no personal data collected or transmitted) without clearly disclosing it here first.
